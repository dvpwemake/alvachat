Technologies

Our proprietary technologies enabling endless possibilities.

  • SDAMS

    (Smart Distributed Account Management System)

    Unique encrypted account on any user’s device, no registration or private information needed

  • P2P DEC

    (Peer-to-peer Decentralized Encrypted Communication)

    All communications are encrypted from end to end to guarantee the security and integrity of the data transmission.

    (URL-free Encrypted Internet File Transfer Mechanism)

    Unlike conventional social apps and websites, all files in AlvaChat system are transferred without any Uniform Resource Locator (URL). It makes the file transfer in AlvaChat uninterceptable and tamper-proof.
